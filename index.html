With three other Product Management students, I tackled the environmental issues problem space. In collaboration with User Experience Design, we conducted market research and came up with two main conclusions: people feel that their individual impact to the big issue is negligible and it is inconvenient and unhabitual to be environmentally conscientious. Through more research, we saw that the agricultural industry and meat production industry contributes to 1/3 of the world's CO2 production.

We ideated an environmental sustainability app, focusing on reducing one's environmental impact through their personal diet. Using 4 main features, we would create a community of users that would be more informed about their personal dietary impact by providing a service that conveniently lets users decide on which days to reduce their meat consumption. Starting small with "Meatless Mondays" can encourage users to adopt this habit, overall reducing his/her personal impact.
